Falco 1.0.0 — first stable release.

Highlights:
- Multi-account, multi-project Hetzner management for Cloud, Robot, DNS
  and S3-compatible Object Storage in one app.
- Privacy-first: TLS 1.3 with root-anchor certificate pinning, hardened
  biometric gate, FLAG_SECURE on every screen, no telemetry.
- Settings hub with Security, Appearance and Language sub-screens —
  including OLED-black theme and five accent colours.
- Donation support via Liberapay.
