Falco is a privacy-respecting Android client for Hetzner Online services.

Manage all four Hetzner APIs from a single secure app:

• Hetzner Cloud — power on/off, reboot, reset, snapshot and rescue your servers; manage volumes, networks, firewalls, floating IPs and load balancers.
• Hetzner Robot — dedicated servers with reset, rescue and Wake-on-LAN; full Storage Box support including snapshots, sub-accounts and password reset; vSwitches and failover IPs.
• Hetzner DNS Console — list zones, create and edit records, bulk import.
• Hetzner Object Storage — browse buckets and objects, upload and download files, generate signed share links.

Security
• No tracking, no ads, no proprietary dependencies — 100% free software (GPL-3.0-or-later).
• Credentials are stored in EncryptedSharedPreferences with the master key bound to the Android Keystore (StrongBox where available) and gated behind biometrics or your device credential.
• TLS 1.3 only, with certificate pinning for every Hetzner endpoint.
• FLAG_SECURE blocks tokens from leaking through screenshots and the Recent-Apps switcher.
• Auto-backup is disabled — secrets never leave the device.

Falco is community software and is not affiliated with Hetzner Online GmbH.
